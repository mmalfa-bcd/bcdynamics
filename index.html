Meet the Founder
Mauricio Malfa

LinkedIn

25+ years at the frontier of

Mauricio Malfa is a senior technology and business executive with over 25 years of experience across some of the world's most influential enterprise technology companies — including IBM, SAP, Microsoft, Oracle, and SAS.

Throughout his career, he has led complex enterprise sales initiatives, strategic partnerships, innovation programs, and business development efforts across Latin America — working closely with C-level executives in financial services, retail, telecommunications, and technology.

His background bridges deep expertise in enterprise software, advanced analytics, and digital transformation with a strong business-first perspective focused on real-world execution and measurable outcomes.

Since 2022, Mauricio has been fully dedicated to the Web3 and Blockchain ecosystem, with a particular focus on stablecoins, tokenization, decentralized infrastructure, and the evolution of digital financial systems. Through BC Dynamics, his mission is clear: help organizations separate noise from signal in a space saturated with hype.

Rather than chasing cycles, he approaches blockchain as strategic infrastructure — capable of improving efficiency, transparency, global payments, digital ownership, and entirely new business models. His work centers on identifying economically viable use cases that generate tangible, lasting impact.
